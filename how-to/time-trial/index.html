How to Play Time Trial:

  1. Objective: Answer as many questions correctly as possible within the time limit.
  2. Interface:
    • You will see a question displayed at the top of the screen.
    • Below the question, there are four answer options labeled A, B, C, and D.
    • Your current score is displayed at the top right corner of the screen.
    • A timer shows the time remaining for the quiz.
  3. Answering Questions:
    • Click on the button corresponding to the answer you believe is correct.
    • If your answer is correct, you'll hear a correct sound effect, and your score will increase by 1.
    • If your answer is incorrect, you'll hear a wrong sound effect, and your score will decrease by 1.
  4. Time Limit:
    • You have 60 seconds to answer as many questions as you can.
    • The timer is displayed at the top left corner of the screen.
    • If you run out of time, the game will end, and your score will be saved as a cookie.
  5. Game End:
    • Once the time is up, the game will automatically redirect you to the finish page.
    • Your final score will be displayed, and you can see how well you did compared to previous attempts.
  6. Restart:
    • If you wish to play again, simply reload the page to start a new round of the quiz.
  7. Scoring:
    • Your score is based on the number of correct answers you provide within the time limit.
    • Try to achieve the highest score possible by answering quickly and accurately.
  8. Enjoy the Challenge:
    • Test your knowledge and reflexes in this quiz game. Have fun and see how well you can perform under pressure!
